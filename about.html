Paper, Paperboard and Pulp

A wide range of paper and paperboard is being used in Writing and Printing Industry, Industrial Packaging, Food Packaging, ETC. on daily basis.

Based on latest observations and researches, the consumption of paper and paperboard in 2021 totaled 408 million tons in 2021 and the total consumption is expected to be rising up to 479 million tons by 2032 (*).

Meanwhile virgin or recycled pulp in different grades, is the main raw material for the manufacturers in the field of Paper, Paperboard, Tissue paper, ETC. and the researches show that the consumption of virgin pulp in the production of paper and paperboard was 171 million tons globally in 2021 (*).

Above numbers and figures show that this business is growing by the minute; and we, The Pacific Blue, have a long-term plan to play an important role in this field.

Using the experience of the team we have gathered to help us connect to the largest most reliable manufacturers all around the world, we are quite positive that we can achieve our goals day by day and provide our customers with the best most qualified products along with competitive prices.

• Paperboard

Paperboard packaging comes in several grades. Solid bleached sulfate (SBS) paperboard contains at least 80% virgin bleached wood pulp.

• Pulp

To make chemically separated pulp, wood chips are essentially cooked at high temperatures in a mixture of water, sodium hydroxide (NaOH), and sodium sulfide (Na2S), a process that breaks it down to a pulp.
